All About Guardin

The real name of guardin is a guy name Nick. He is about 20 years old, he first started on soundcloud. He later on start posting music on Youtube. From that on he manage to get around 20 thousand subscribers. The type of music that Nick does is emo music like emo rap or emo punk music. He lives in New York City, and he has been in many concerts and places. He has been invited to join many tours. His songs would be songs about break ups or something that shouldn't be said here. He makes most of his own beats, and occaionally plays the ukleule.More info and personal likes On Soundcloud, he has over 100 music done, with most of it over 20k listened. He started to gain fame in 2017.

I personally listened to him for a real long time from 2013. He has done countless collaboration with many others personal favorites and other singers from 9tails, Yung Van, guccihighwaters, and flowars. He first started off with some mellow beats, but as time past on he starts to rap/sing with them. His most popular album, Lacuna, is his album which gain his fame, and the start of his tours and music life. He is very supportive with his fan base, and he has many social media platform from Instagram to Twitter, and three music platform; Spotify, Soundcloud, and Apple Music. He has many merch with many of his albums. His motto, or rather favorite quote is "make your dreams your reality." Guardin is one of my favorite singer, he doesn't really i would say inspire me, but rather make me happy about he feels the same way we (listeners) feels.
